We are a group of farmers, technocrats and IT professionals. This unique business model was conceptualized to benefit customers and farmer by bypassing middlemen. At the same time we are committed to the freshness, pureness and healthiness of our products. We train farmers affiliated to us for minimum use of pesticides and other environment friendly measures like water conservation, judicious use of fertilizers and manure for soil health and biological controls of the pests.

We endeavour to provide fresh vegetables from direct farm to the customer. For that we maintain a system among a group of farmers and this website.
